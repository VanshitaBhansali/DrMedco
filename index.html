Go Beyond the Claims Processing : We Deliver Revenue Growth.

From Tailored Billing Workflows to Intelligent RCM Experts, we provide the dedicated staff that allows your practice to thrive.

RCM Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

The complete process of tracking a patient encounter from registration and appointment scheduling to the final payment and closure of the account. It ensures practices get paid correctly and on time.

Medical Billing Services

The overall service of submitting claims to insurance payers and patients, following up on denials, and ensuring proper reimbursement for services provided by the doctor.

Credentialing

The detailed process of verifying a provider's qualifications (education, licenses, certifications) and applying for them to be officially enrolled as an in-network provider with specific insurance companies.
